About Me

Christopher Robert ‘Chris’ Evans is an American director and actor.
He is famous for his superhero roles in movies; Captain America and the Fantastic Four series.

Career

Chris began his acting career with TV series, 'Opposite Sex' in 2000. He debuted in
films with 'Not Another Teen Movie' in 2001. Evans landed lead roles in 'The
Perfect Score' and 'The Cellular'. He acted in Dirk Wittenborn’s film, 'Fierce People'.
Evans gained popularity as a superhero, Human Torch in 'Fantastic Four' and
in its sequel 'Fantastic Four - Rise of the Silver Surfer'. Chris played a role in
Danny Boyle’s 'Sunshine'. Chris Evans won critical acclaim for his roles
in Fantastic Four, Captain America and The Avengers. He played the lead
role in the movie 'Snowpiercer'.
